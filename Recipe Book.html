Tomato Soup

  • Time: 18 min (Lesser time then completing your assignment)
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato 4 pieces
Ginger& Garlic 3 pieces each
Species 1 tablespoon each
Veggies of your choice 2 pieces each

Step 1:Heat butter in a pan and put ginger - garlic saute for 1 minutes
Step 2: Then add all chopped vegies one by one and saute them for 3-4 minutes.
Step 3: Add 3 cup of water and soaked cournflour mix well without any lumps.
Step 4: Boil it for 2 -3 min on midium flame.
Step 5: Add salt and black pepper powder and mix well.
Step 6: Switch off the gas and serve hot.

Paneer Carrot Paranthas

  • Time: 18 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 300 gm
Paneer 200 gm
Species 1 tablespoon each
Carrot 50 gm
Ghee 2 teaspoon

Step 1:Mix all milk and make a smooth pliable dough.
Step 2: Cover with a lid and keep aside for 5 minutes.
Step 3: In another bowl mix all the ingredients for the filling.
Step 4: Now take a portion of dough and fill the Paneer mixture, make a ball, and roll into a parantha of medium thickness
Step 5:Toast on tawa adding little ghee on both sides, till cooked and golden Brown in colour.
Step 6: Serve with ketchup or mayonnaise..gas and serve hot.


Chettinad Potato

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Boiled Potatoes 4 pieces
Ginger& Garlic 3 pieces each
Species 1 tablespoon each
Small pieces of Onions 2 pieces each

Step 1:Peel potatoes and keep aside
Step 2: Heat oil in a wok
Step 3: Crackle mustard seeds and urad dal
Step 4: Add Kari Patta soute ginger garlic paste and chopped onion
Step 5: Add potatoes and soute for 1 to 2minutes and mix all dry masala
Step 6: Cook with lid on slow flame for 10 minutes
Step 7: Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with roti or poori
